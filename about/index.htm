About

(More like a backstory)

TheBestPH is a middle schooler who likes creating stuff. He was first inspired by a lot of Youtubers when he was in his elementary days. He then joined youtube on May 21, 2018 and started uploading videos. He reached 100 subscribers on November 2018! On Jan 1, 2019, He stopped uploading videos for a while and came back after 3 months where he uploaded his current most viewed video on his channel; A roblox studio tutorial!

After TheBestPH uploaded bunch of gacha and roblox videos, He again started playing minecraft. He uploaded a new video about an adventure map in MCPE. Then TheBestPH has a new inspiration while playing some maps. He decided to create his own minecraft map.

Right now, he has more than 700 subs on youtube, created 3 map and 1 texture packs for minecraft, and trying to be more popular!
